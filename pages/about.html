Meet Janae

“You deserve to have love and happiness! You need a counselor who really knows how to help you to develop a close relationship with those you love, someone who specializes in creating secure bonds and helping you know how to keep them even in the difficult times in life. I can help you achieve your goal.”

~ Janae Harman

Licensed Professional Counselor

For over 10 years I have been helping couples and families to discover what is going wrong in their relationships. I can help you understand the rut you have fallen into with your loved ones and help you see a way out to an easier, happier path, one that keeps you feeling close and bridges the distance and contention you currently feel. The negative pattern you and your loved one are currently stuck in is hard to understand. It’s so very confusing! This pattern happens so fast and emotions run so high that you feel confused and discouraged. You wonder how you got there and feel hopeless and powerless to change it. I know how to help you make sense of that pattern or cycle so you can see a way to reach that loved one, bringing them closer, soothing your worries, anxieties, depression, and misery. Helping you feel closer, happy, excited, and well!

~ Janae Harman
