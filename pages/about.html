Meet Janae

“When you have tried everything you know and you feel stuck with no alternatives, I stay with you while we work together to find solutions that fit your life and beliefs.”

~ Janae Harman

Licensed Professional Counselor

Sometimes in life we need another perspective, a guide, to help find our way through to the other side of the problem we are dealing with – someone neutral, with fresh ideas, and specific strategies. I’ve made it my business to understand human relationships and processes- especially how people work, so that I can help people like you, those wanting life to be better.

Our emotions are information. We aren’t taught how to use that information to best help, inform, and guide us. And sometimes the emotions just feel overwhelming- whether it is your child’s anger, your fear of being left, or the deep, deep sadness that you feel, it is information and I can help you decode it.

When you’ve run out of ideas and feel there are no options but you still want to feel different in your life, in your relationships, with your loved ones, call me. I will help you through.

~ Janae Harman
