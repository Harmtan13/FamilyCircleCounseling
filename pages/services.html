Grief Recovery

There has been a change in your life, one that you didn’t want. You feel devastated by it. You desperately try to “get over it.” Small things trigger a fresh wave of grief.. a smell,a look or perhaps a song…within seconds you are flung into a time machine and are transported back to that moment when time stood still and the world crashed at your feet.

I can guide you through the confusion. I know the way through the sadness so you can once again take in the joy of the moments that come along, so you can embrace the future that is there for you. You can feel vibrant again, you just need the right steps to get there and I know what those steps are. Break free from the agony. Let’s get started!
