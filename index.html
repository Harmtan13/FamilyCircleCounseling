Family Circle Counseling

Strengthening You by Strengthening Relationships

You are designed as a human being to desire and need another person, one you can love, who loves you, who will be there for you no matter what. When that doesn’t happen nothing seems right, life feels draining. You can’t focus, you can’t motivate yourself, and you question if you are of worth. You just want to feel loved, give love, and to know you are important to at least ONE person. You try to not want that connection because it hurts so bad when it’s not working and pretend you are happy without it. But, you’re not. Let me help you reclaim your passion for life and help you discover what isn’t working so you can have that loving connection that makes your world go round.
